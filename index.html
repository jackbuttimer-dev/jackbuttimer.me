About

A bit about me

I’m combining hands-on technical work, client support and a growing interest in UI, layout and user experience.

I’ve spent the last few years working in technical and service-based roles – building, fixing and looking after systems for other people. Along the way I realised I really enjoy making tools feel clean, simple and easy to use.

This portfolio is where I’m learning by doing: shipping small projects, cleaning up layouts, and slowly moving from “copy/paste” to understanding how everything fits together.

Outside of tech I’m into coffee (hence Jupiters), building side projects, and studying health & nutrition.
