About Holisti

From lived experience to gentle, structured support.

I’m Jack, a nutrition and health coach in training with a background in autoimmune and digestive issues myself. Holisti grew out of years of trial, error and research — and the realisation that most people don’t need more noise, they need calm, evidence-informed guidance.

I know what it’s like to feel stuck in your own body.

For years, I dealt with reflux, gut pain and the anxiety that comes with never knowing how your body will react. Tracking symptoms, trying protocols, reading research — it all helped, but only once I connected the dots between food, stress, sleep, nervous system and lifestyle did things really start to shift.

Holisti is here to give you the kind of support I wish I’d had: someone in your corner who understands the science, but also the reality of real life, work, family and stress.

Gut & digestive focus Autoimmune-aware approach Real-world, flexible plans Compassion-first coaching

*I’m currently completing a professional nutrition & health coaching diploma. Coaching is educational and supportive and does not replace medical care.

  • We go root-cause, not quick-fix. We look at digestion, stress, sleep, blood sugar balance and habits — not just your plate.
  • We move at your nervous system’s pace. No “all-or-nothing” overhauls. Tiny, doable shifts that add up.
  • We build skills, not dependency. The goal is that you leave coaching with tools you can use for life.
